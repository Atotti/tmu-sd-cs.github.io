Toru Yamaguchi Laboratory

Professor Toru Yamaguchi

Intelligent System / Community-centric System / Intention Understanding / Intelligent Information Processing

Yamaguchi’s research focuses on robot which supporting our daily living with human status monitoring system. Not just a one-to-one relationship between human and robot, the robot is aimed to be as a member of community to build the bond among people with wide perspective on the basis of informatic science.

It is easy to have an interaction with robot in various places today, just merely by curiosity and interest. However, his research goal is to build a useful and reliable robot.

  • Laboratory website

With the return of AI technology in the limelight; social robot can be seen in various place and robot serving as receptionist has been commercialized. While technology breakthrough keeps going on, “A robot will take over human jobs.” has become a hot topic. However, will all jobs be taken over by robots?

Yamaguchi’s research aims to build the connection among human community by utilizing individual skill and encouraging mutual support. To pursue this target, his research focuses on the communication system integrating human sensing technology, human behavior understanding to extract individual interest and skill/weakness by conversation technology, and facilitate to connect matching pair who can swap skill or complementary interest.

Moreover, the researches for the communication system not just support the vitality of community dependent environment, it is also practicable to smooth the association with people far away by using telepresence system.
