Assistant Professor Kenta Tsukatsune

Assistant Professor Kenta Tsukatsune

Behavior and Mentality of SNS Users / Social Statistics / Onomastics

I have been researching human behavior and mentality, using data analysis methods developed in the field of social science. Especially, in recent years, I have focused on classifying the behavior and mentality of SNS users and identifying the factors that determine these classes. I am also conducting research to clarify information characteristics and social functions of proper names, such as person names and place names.
